Network Administrator

Description
Requirement
Key Responsibilities

Essential

  • Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Networking, or related field
  • Strong understanding of LAN/WAN, routers, switches, and firewalls
  • Knowledge of network protocols and security principles
  • Problem-solving and troubleshooting skills

Preferable

  • Professional certifications such as CompTIA Network+ or Cisco CCNA
  • Experience with cloud networking and virtualization technologies
  • Familiarity with VPN setup and wireless configurations
  • Knowledge of network monitoring tools (e.g., Wireshark, SolarWinds)
