Data Analyst

Description
Requirement
Key Responsibilities

Essential

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
  • Proficiency in Excel, SQL, or data analysis tools (Python or R)
  • Ability to clean, organize, and interpret large datasets
  • Strong analytical and critical-thinking skills

Preferable

  • Experience with data visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau)
  • Knowledge of statistical modeling or predictive analytics
  • Understanding of databases and business intelligence systems
  • Prior experience working in a data-driven environment
